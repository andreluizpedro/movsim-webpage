MovSim v1.0

Description:

Version 1.0 is a one lane simulator. Developed primarily with the purpose to support the book on traffic flow dynamics. In particular the main chapters 10 to 14 which form the basis of microscopic traffic modeling with various models.

A demonstration of MovSim version 1.0 can be viewed at www.verkehrsdynamik.de/simulation.shtml. The applet produces a fact sheet to the microscopic traffic models with two scenarios and lets you change the parameters of every model interactively. A great tool if you want to understand the implemented models.
