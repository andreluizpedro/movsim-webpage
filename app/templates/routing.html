Objective:

Switch the routing recommendation to use the capacity of the two roads optimally.

Click anywhere on the green canvas to applay routing.

What makes the game interesting:

Optimal routing is quite complex.

Externally given:

A fixed traffic demand curve with a "rush-hour" peak. The main road ("freeway") has a road works bottleneck. At peak demand, the inflow exceeds the freeway capacity. It is set such that only the best player should manage to avoid all breakdowns. The capacity of the "village bottleneck" is half the capacity of one freeway lane (or less).
